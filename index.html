About Me

I'm a Senior Cloud Engineer with deep expertise in Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI), AWS, and GCP — building and operating enterprise-scale systems across all three major cloud platforms. My work centers on making complex, multi-cloud environments run faster, more reliably, and more cost-effectively.

I also work directly with external clients — demoing new features, supporting onboarding, and translating complex multi-cloud architectures into real business value. I care deeply about building systems that are both technically sound and aligned with real business outcomes.
